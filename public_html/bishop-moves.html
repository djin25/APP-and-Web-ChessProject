The Bishop:

  • Bbishop
  • Bbishop
  • Wbishop
  • Wbishop
  • The bishop moves diagonally, and cannot move straight. There are two kinds of bishops--light-squared bishops and dark-squared bishops, based on the color of the square that the bishop starts on. Because of the checkered pattern of the board and the way in which the bishop moves, a light-sqaured bishop can only ever move to a light sqaure, and a dark-squared bishop can only ever move to a dark square.
